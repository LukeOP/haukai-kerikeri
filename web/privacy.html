Haukai Restaurant Privacy Statement

We collect personal information from you, including information about your:

  • name
  • contact information

    • We collect your personal information in order to:

  • keep in contact with you regarding your reservations and enquiries.

    • Providing information is optional.

    If you choose not to enter your contact details, we'll be unable to make reservations for you online or respond to any enquiries.


    We keep your information safe by storing it securely and only allowing certain staff to access it .

    Your information will not be shared with any third party except where required under New Zealand legislation


    You have the right to ask for a copy of any personal information we hold about you, and to ask for it to be corrected if you think it is wrong. If you’d like to ask for a copy of your information, or to have it corrected, please contact us at +64 9 401 4019, or 1 Island Street, Kerikeri, Northland, New Zealand.
